*Plain-language summary of benefits and expectations for beta participants*
*These terms are incorporated into the abcAssess Terms of Service Section 6.10*
 
---
 
### Thank you for participating in the abcAssess beta. This document summarizes what you're entitled to as a beta participant and what's expected of you. It's written in plain language — the full legal terms are in Section 6.10 of the abcAssess Terms of Service at abcassess.app.
 
---
 
## What You Get
 
#### During Beta
- **Full free access** to all features in abcAssess for the duration of the beta period
- No credit card required, no subscription needed

#### After Launch (Fall 2026)
- **One free year** of full access following public launch — same features as paid subscribers, no charge
- To claim your free year: complete the end-of-beta survey by the deadline Danielle sends you. If you miss the deadline, reach out to beta@abcassess.app — she'll work with you.

#### Ongoing — Founding Member Rate
- After your free year, you'll never pay full price
- **20% off the current subscription price, forever** — on both Monthly and Annual plans
- If prices go up in the future, your discount goes up with them — you always pay 20% less than the standard rate
- Right now that means: $4/month (instead of $5) or $40/year (instead of $50)

#### If You're Part of a Beta Center
- Every teacher at your center who actively participated and completes the survey gets their own free year and Founding Member rate
- When center licensing becomes available, your center receives a **permanent 20% discount** off the center license rate

---
 
## What's Expected of You
 
#### Use It For Real
Use abcAssess with your actual students during real assessment windows. The more you use it, the more valuable your feedback is — and the more you'll get out of it.
 
#### Explore Beyond Your Daily Routine
Try features you might not reach for every day — reports, custom assessments, co-teacher invitations, backups, settings. The only way bugs and problems get found is if teachers actually use these features.
 
#### Tell Danielle Everything
Send feedback whenever something occurs to you — broken things, confusing things, ideas, wishes, vague feelings that something isn't quite right. No message is too small. If you think of five things in one day, send five messages.
 
- **In-app:** Beta Feedback button
- **Email:** beta@abcassess.app
  
#### Complete the End-of-Beta Survey
Before launch, Danielle will send a short survey (about 5–10 minutes). Completing it is what confirms your free year. If you didn't use abcAssess much during beta, fill it out anyway — that feedback matters too.
 
---
 
## What's Not Expected of You
 
- You don't need to use abcAssess a minimum number of times
- You don't need to be an expert or know everything about the app
- You don't need to write formal reports or structured feedback
- You don't need to be positive — honest is what matters

---
 
## A Few Things to Know
 
**Your data is safe.** The same encryption and privacy protections in the launch version are in place right now. Beta status doesn't affect how your students' data is handled.
 
**Things will change.** The interface, features, and workflows may change during beta. That's intentional. If something looks different than you remember, that's probably a good sign.
 
**The Founding Member rate is non-transferable.** It applies to your individual account only and cannot be applied to a new account if you create one later.
 
**The center discount applies to your center's account**, not individual teacher accounts, when center licensing becomes available.
 
---
 
## Questions
 
Reach out to Danielle directly at beta@abcassess.app. She reads everything.
