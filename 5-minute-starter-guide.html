Your First 5 Minutes with abcAssess

⏱ 5 minutes. Faster than convincing someone to put their shoes on.

1
Create Your Account
~1 min

Go to abcassess.app and tap Sign Up. Email, Google, or Apple — whatever's fastest.

14 days free, full access, no card needed. Just go.

Your Phone Is Enough

Playground, hallway, snack time — abcAssess fits in your pocket and goes wherever you do.

2
Set Up Your Class
~1 min
  1. Name your class and pick a grade level
  2. Add students — first name's all you need

Tap Save & Finish and you're on your Dashboard.

Built for Every Learner

Support your late bloomers, challenge your high flyers — customize per student, anytime.

3
Run an Assessment
~2 min

Tap a student's Start Assessment button. Try Uppercase Letters first.

One card at a time — tap Known or Unknown. Saves as you go.

Use the Keyboard

→ for Known, ← for Unknown. Same face, every card — kids can't tell which ones they missed.

4
Check Results
~30 sec

Last card, and results pop up — score, proficiency level, exactly which items to revisit.

Nothing to write down. Already saved and ready for reports.

That's It!

A real, saved assessment, no spreadsheet in sight. The Reports page turns this into a PDF in seconds.

Good to Know
Offline's fine

No Wi-Fi? Keep assessing. Everything syncs the moment you're back online.

Pause, don't panic

Need a beat? Hit pause — a quick unlock code keeps little hands from un-pausing it for you.

→ Build your own assessments Assessment Settings
