## 1. Purpose & Scope This DPA is between abcAssess ("Provider") and the educational institution or educator ("Subscriber") using abcAssess. It governs the processing of "Student Data" which may include names, birth months, and assessment scores.


---

## 2. Compliance (FERPA & COPPA)

* **School Official Status:** Provider agrees that for the purposes of FERPA, it is performing an institutional service which the Subscriber would otherwise use employees to perform.
* **Direct Control:** Provider operates under the direct control of the Subscriber regarding the use and maintenance of Education Records.
* **No Commercial Use:** Provider will never sell, rent, or use Student Data for targeted advertising or marketing.

---

## 3. Technical Stack & Sub-processors To provide the service, Provider uses the following secure infrastructure:

* **Vercel Inc.:** Frontend Hosting (SOC2 Compliant)
* **Railway:** Backend Services (SOC2 Type II)
* **MongoDB Atlas:** Data Storage (Encrypted at rest/transit)
* **Resend:** Transactional Email (Support/Notifications)

---

## 4. Data Security & Deletion

* **Encryption:** All data is encrypted via TLS 1.3 in transit and AES-256 at rest.
* **Deletion:** Subscriber may request full deletion of student records at any time. Provider will purge data from production systems within 30 days of a verified request.
