## 1. Purpose & Scope

#### This Data Processing Addendum ("DPA") forms a legally binding agreement between abcAssess ("Provider") and the educational institution, licensed childcare center, or individual educator ("Subscriber") utilizing the abcAssess platform. This agreement strictly governs the processing of text-based classroom data, student first names, birth month/year arrays, and associated raw assessment scoring metrics recorded within the Service.

---

## 2. Legal & Compliance Frameworks (FERPA & COPPA)

* **School Official Exception (FERPA):** Pursuant to the Family Educational Rights and Privacy Act (FERPA), Provider acknowledges that it operates strictly as a "School Official" with a legitimate educational interest. Provider performs an institutional data management utility that the Subscriber would otherwise utilize internal staff to execute.
* **Direct Custodial Control:** Provider operates under the direct operational control of the Subscriber regarding the maintenance, access parameters, and retention schedules of student educational profiles logged within the system.
* **Commercial Exploitation Prohibition (COPPA):** In absolute compliance with the Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA), Provider guarantees that student information will never be sold, leased, rented, or leveraged for tracking, profiling, machine learning model training, or targeted behavioral advertisements.

---

## 3. Verified Sub-Processor Architecture

#### To deliver secure, edge-optimized calculation and reporting operations, Provider utilizes a restricted suite of enterprise infrastructure sub-processors. Each entity is contractually bound to maintain strict security baselines:

* **Vercel Inc.:** Secure User Interface Frontend Architecture Hosting (SOC 2 Type II Compliant).
* **Railway:** Application Environment Processing, Secret Variable Vaulting, and API Backend Management (SOC 2 Type II Compliant).
* **MongoDB Atlas:** Multi-Tenant Encrypted Document Database Cloud Storage (SOC 2 / ISO 27001 Certified).
* **Resend:** Transactional System Outbound Mail Relay Operations (SOC 2 Type II Compliant).

---

## 4. Operational Media & Privacy Restraints

#### The platform permits the Subscriber to upload customized visual assets (e.g., JPEG instructional graphics) and record vocalized teacher prompt clips to format personalized assessment categories (such as multilingual phonemic drills). 

* **Prohibited Content:** The Subscriber is strictly prohibited from uploading, recording, or storing any student-facing biometric data, including student profile photos, childhood video frames, or child voiceprint recordings. All multimedia elements hosted inside the Service libraries must remain strictly instructional.

---

## 5. Security Architecture & Data Erasure Bounds

* **Field-Level Encryption Safeguards:** abcAssess utilizes Client-Side Field-Level Encryption (CSFLE). Highly sensitive student identifiers and score arrays are cryptographically blinded at the application layer using an AES-256 bit Master Key structure before hitting cloud databases, rendering data unreadable to database personnel or administrators.
* **Cascade Deletion Operations:** Subscriber maintains autonomous control to delete files, specific classroom rosters, or entire account profiles at any time. Upon receiving an account termination signal, Provider triggers a "Cascade Purge" to clear all linked indices from active production systems within thirty (30) days.
* **Custody & Transfer Exemptions:** The erasure mandate outlined above explicitly excludes student records where the Subscriber has chosen to execute a shared co-teaching cluster or initiated an intentional roster transfer to a verified receiving educator's classroom history account. Once transferred, data custody falls to the active controller.
