## Our Commitment 

### At abcAssess, we believe every teacher should have access to high-quality assessment tools. we are committed to ensuring digital accessibility for people with disabilities and are continually improving the user experience for everyone.

---

## Conformance Status 

### The Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) defines requirements for designers and developers to improve accessibility. abcAssess is currently Partially Conformant with WCAG 2.1 Level AA.

### Accessibility Features

* **Semantic HTML:** We use proper heading structures (H1, H2) to help screen reader navigation.
* **Keyboard Operability:** Core assessment buttons are navigable via the Tab key.
* **Contrast:** We aim for a minimum contrast ratio of 4.5:1 for all critical text.
*	**Alt Text:** Non-decorative images include descriptive text for assistive technology.

### Known Limitations

* Some complex data visualization charts for reports may not yet have full text-equivalents.
* We are working on adding accessible CSV exports for all data views by Q3 2026.
