Welcome to the abcAssess Beta
abcassess.app · beta@abcassess.app

Hi — I'm so glad you're here. Genuinely.

I'm Danielle. I'm a Pre-K teacher, not a tech company. There's no team behind this, no company, no investors — just me, a classroom full of four-year-olds, and a lot of late nights. I built abcAssess because I needed it and couldn't find it anywhere else. After years of flashcards, paper packets, and spending more time on assessment paperwork than with my kids, I decided to try building something better.

What you're using right now is the result of that. Built in a real Pre-K classroom, tested with real students, and refined based on what actually works. And now I need teachers like you to help me make sure it's as good as it should be before it goes out to the world.

What "Beta" Means

abcAssess is fully functional — you can use it with real students right now. But as with anything in development, you may encounter rough edges, missing features, or things that don't quite work the way they should yet.

What That Means

  • Some features described in the User Guide may not be fully implemented yet — I'm building continuously
  • The interface may change as I improve it — if something looks different than you remember, that's intentional
  • You may occasionally encounter bugs — please report them when you do, that's exactly how I fix them

No Need to Worry

  • Your data is fully protected — the same encryption and privacy safeguards from the launch version are already in place
  • You're a valued early adopter — your real classroom experience is directly shaping this product
  • Everything carries forward — your classes, students, and assessment history move automatically into the full version at launch. Nothing to redo.

Your Beta Access Is Free

You have full access to everything in abcAssess at no cost for the duration of the beta period. When abcAssess launches publicly in Fall 2026, your free access continues for a full year as a thank-you for being part of this. This applies to every active teacher at participating beta centers too.

After your free year — Founding Member rate: Beta participants never pay full price. You'll be offered 20% off whatever the current subscription price is, for as long as you subscribe. Right now that means $4/month instead of $5, or $40/year instead of $50. Beta centers will also receive a permanent 20% discount on center licensing once that's available.

To confirm your free year: before launch, I'll send a short end-of-beta survey — about 5–10 minutes. Completing that survey is all you need to do. If you didn't use it as much as you hoped, I still want to hear from you — that feedback is just as valuable.

What I Need From You

The most valuable thing you can do is use abcAssess the way you would any tool — in your real classroom, with your real students, during your real assessment windows. And then tell me everything. I mean that. About everything.

  • Something's broken or not working right? Tell me.
  • Something's confusing or hard to figure out? Tell me.
  • Something works great and you don't want me to touch it? Tell me.
  • You keep thinking "wow, this is great but I wish it would…"? Tell me.
  • You have an idea — even a small one, even an obvious one? Tell me.

No message is too small. No idea is too half-formed. If you think of five things in one day, send five messages. That's exactly what I'm hoping for.

Use the Beta Feedback button in the app anytime, or email me directly at beta@abcassess.app. I'm the one reading it. There's no team — it's just me.

Known Limitations in Beta

A few things still in progress: light mode polish (dark mode is the recommended experience for now), a handful of formatting issues here and there, and a planned redesign of the tracking page. The User Guide reflects the full intended product, so some sections describe features still being refined — that's intentional, so you know where I'm headed.

How to Get Help

  • Beta feedback and questions: beta@abcassess.app
  • Bugs and feature ideas: Beta Feedback button in the app

I personally read every message — because there's no one else to do it, and honestly I wouldn't want it any other way.

Thank You

Genuinely — thank you. Building something new is uncertain work, and having teachers willing to trust me with their classrooms during that process is not something I take lightly.

If you're a teacher who cares deeply about your students and just wants a better way to do this — you're exactly who I built this for.

Welcome. Let's make it great together.
