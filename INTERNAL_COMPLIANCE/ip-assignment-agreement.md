## RECITALS
 
WHEREAS, the Assignor is the original creator, lead developer, and sole owner of all proprietary rights, title, and interests in and to the educational assessment software application known as abcAssess, including but not limited to its source code, database architectures, user interface designs, website assets, documentation, and associated trademarks (collectively, the "Intellectual Property"); and
 
WHEREAS, the Assignor conceived, designed, and directed the development of the Intellectual Property through substantial and sustained human creative effort, including the authorship of detailed and specific prompts, iterative testing and evaluation of outputs, selective curation and rejection of generated content, hands-on modification and refinement of code and design elements, and the original conception of the underlying educational assessment frameworks and product architecture; and
 
WHEREAS, in the course of developing the Intellectual Property, the Assignor utilized AI-assisted software development tools as instrumentalities of her creative process, in a manner analogous to the use of compilers, code libraries, or other software development tools, and the resulting work product reflects the Assignor's original ideas, creative direction, and intellectual vision; and
 
WHEREAS, the Assignor has formed the Company to operate, manage, and scale the abcAssess application; and
 
WHEREAS, the Assignor desires to assign and transfer one hundred percent (100%) of her global rights, title, and interests in and to the Intellectual Property to the Company, and the Company desires to accept such assignment.
 
NOW, THEREFORE, in consideration of the mutual covenants contained herein and other good and valuable consideration, the receipt and sufficiency of which are hereby acknowledged, the parties agree as follows:
 
---
 
## ARTICLE I: Assignment of Intellectual Property
 
### 1.1 Transfer of Rights
 
The Assignor hereby irrevocably sells, assigns, transfers, and conveys to the Company, its successors, and assigns, all global rights, title, and interests worldwide in and to the Intellectual Property. This assignment includes, without limitation:
 
- The core software application, repository codebase, algorithms, data structures, and all updates, derivative works, and improvements related to abcAssess, whether created before or after the Effective Date.
- All copyrights, trade secrets, trademarks, service marks, logos, brand names, and domain names associated with abcAssess (including abcassess.app).
- All design layouts, graphics, documentation, teacher/student assessment frameworks, and marketing assets.
- All proprietary prompt libraries, AI interaction logs, development notes, and creative direction documentation generated in the course of developing the Intellectual Property.
- The right to sue, counter-claim, and recover damages for any past, present, or future infringement or misappropriation of the Intellectual Property.
### 1.2 Future Improvements
 
This assignment expressly includes all derivative works, updates, enhancements, modifications, and improvements to the Intellectual Property created after the Effective Date, whether developed by the Assignor, Company employees, contractors, or through any tools or technologies including AI-assisted development tools. All such future work product shall automatically vest in and belong exclusively to the Company upon creation.
 
### 1.3 Consideration
 
As full consideration for the assignment of the Intellectual Property, the Company acknowledges the initial issuance of one hundred percent (100%) of the Membership Units in abcAssess LLC to the Assignor, as detailed in the Company's Operating Agreement.
 
---
 
## ARTICLE II: Human Authorship and AI-Assisted Development
 
### 2.1 Disclosure of AI-Assisted Development
 
The Assignor discloses that portions of the Intellectual Property, including elements of the source code and design assets, were developed with the assistance of AI-powered software development tools. The Assignor affirms that such tools were used solely as instrumentalities of her creative process and that the Intellectual Property as a whole reflects substantial human creative authorship.
 
### 2.2 Nature of Human Creative Contribution
 
The Assignor's human creative contributions to the Intellectual Property include, without limitation, the following:
 
- Original conception and design of the abcAssess educational assessment system, including its underlying pedagogical frameworks, product architecture, and user experience vision.
- Authorship of detailed, specific, and iterative prompts used to direct AI-assisted development tools, which prompts reflect the Assignor's independent creative judgment and technical expertise.
- Sustained iterative review, testing, evaluation, and modification of AI-generated outputs, including the selective acceptance, rejection, and refinement of generated code and design elements.
- Integration, arrangement, and curation of all components of the codebase into a coherent, functional, and original software application.
- Ongoing creative and technical decision-making throughout the development process, including architectural choices, feature prioritization, and quality standards.
The Assignor's creative process is documented through development histories, prompt records, version control logs, and related materials, which the Assignor agrees to preserve and make available to the Company upon request.
 
### 2.3 Trade Secret Protection
 
Regardless of the copyright status of any individual component of the Intellectual Property, the Assignor and the Company agree that the Intellectual Property in its entirety — including its architecture, codebase, algorithms, prompt libraries, data structures, and development methodologies — constitutes a trade secret of the Company under applicable state and federal trade secret law, including the North Dakota Trade Secrets Act and the federal Defend Trade Secrets Act (18 U.S.C. § 1836 et seq.). The Company shall take reasonable measures to maintain the confidentiality of the Intellectual Property as a trade secret.
 
### 2.4 Open Source and Third-Party Components
 
The Assignor represents and warrants that any open-source software, third-party libraries, or licensed components incorporated into the Intellectual Property are identified to the best of the Assignor's knowledge and are used in compliance with their respective licenses. Such third-party components are not included in this assignment to the extent they are subject to licenses that preclude assignment, but all of the Assignor's rights in and to such components, including any modifications thereto, are hereby assigned to the Company.
 
### 2.5 Moral Rights Waiver
 
To the fullest extent permitted by applicable law, the Assignor hereby irrevocably waives any and all moral rights, rights of attribution, rights of integrity, and any similar rights in and to the Intellectual Property in favor of the Company and its successors and assigns.
 
---
 
## ARTICLE III: Representations and Warranties
 
### 3.1 Ownership
 
The Assignor represents and warrants that she is the sole author and legal owner of the Intellectual Property, that the Intellectual Property is free and clear of any liens, encumbrances, or third-party claims, and that she has the full legal right and authority to execute this transfer.
 
### 3.2 No Infringement
 
To the best of the Assignor's knowledge, the Intellectual Property does not infringe upon the copyrights, patents, trademarks, or proprietary rights of any outside third party, except as may arise from the nature of AI-assisted development as disclosed in Article II.
 
### 3.3 No Prior Assignments
 
The Assignor represents and warrants that she has not previously assigned, licensed, pledged, or otherwise encumbered any rights in the Intellectual Property to any third party, and that no other person or entity has any claim to or interest in the Intellectual Property.
 
---
 
## ARTICLE IV: Further Assurances
 
### 4.1 Cooperation
 
The Assignor covenants and agrees to perform any further acts and sign any additional legal documents (such as state or federal trademark or copyright registrations) that the Company may reasonably request in the future to fully evidence, perfect, or record the Company's sole ownership of the Intellectual Property. This includes cooperating with any copyright registration strategy developed by the Company in consultation with intellectual property counsel, including applications asserting human authorship of the Intellectual Property.
 
### 4.2 Documentation Preservation
 
The Assignor agrees to preserve, to the extent reasonably practicable, all records of the creative development process, including prompt histories, AI interaction logs, version control histories, development notes, and communications relating to the design and development of the Intellectual Property. Such records support the establishment of human authorship and trade secret status of the Intellectual Property and shall be made available to the Company upon reasonable request.

## ARTICLE V: Miscellaneous Provisions
 
### 5.1 Entire Agreement
 
This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement between the parties with respect to the subject matter hereof and supersedes all prior and contemporaneous agreements, understandings, negotiations, and discussions, whether oral or written, relating to the ownership of the Intellectual Property.
 
### 5.2 Binding Effect
 
This Agreement shall be binding upon, and inure to the benefit of, the parties hereto and their respective heirs, legal representatives, successors, and permitted assigns.
 
### 5.3 Governing Law
 
This Agreement shall be governed by, construed, and enforced in accordance with the laws of the State of North Dakota, without regard to its conflict of law principles.
 
### 5.4 Severability
 
If any provision of this Agreement is held to be invalid, illegal, or unenforceable, the validity and enforceability of the remaining provisions shall not be affected.
 
### 5.5 Amendments
 
This Agreement may not be amended, modified, or supplemented except by a written instrument signed by both parties.
