# COPPA School Authorization & Consent Waiver
Product: abcAssess Assessment Platform

The undersigned Educational Institution ("School") hereby authorizes its administrative staff and lead classroom practitioners to utilize the abcAssess platform for student performance monitoring, chronological age calculations, and report generation.

Pursuant to the Children’s Online Privacy Protection Rule (COPPA), the School acknowledges that:
1. abcAssess operates under a strict Data Minimization policy, collecting zero student photos, video captures, or voiceprint recordings.
2. abcAssess utilizes Client-Side Field Level Encryption (CSFLE) to blind student records from database personnel.
3. The School elects to act as the legal agent for parents or guardians, granting authorization for the collection of student first names and birth months solely for internal educational progress evaluations.
