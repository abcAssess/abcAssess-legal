## Section 2: Scope of Authorization
 
### 2.1 Authorized Purpose
The School hereby authorizes its qualified educational staff — including classroom teachers, intervention specialists, and designated instructional support personnel — to use the abcAssess platform for the following purposes:
 
- Recording and tracking student assessment performance for internal educational evaluation.
- Calculating student chronological age for the purpose of selecting age-appropriate assessment tools.
- Generating individual student reports, class-wide summaries, and progress records for use by authorized school personnel.
- Transferring student records between verified classrooms within the School where educationally appropriate, such as during grade-level transitions.
### 2.2 Authorized Users
The School authorizes only the following categories of personnel to access and use abcAssess on behalf of the School:
 
- Licensed classroom teachers employed by or contracted with the School.
- Instructional aides or assistant teachers acting under the direct supervision of a licensed teacher.
- School administrators with a legitimate educational interest in student assessment data.
The School is responsible for ensuring that access is limited to authorized personnel and that all authorized users have been informed of their obligations under this Authorization, the abcAssess Terms of Service, and applicable privacy law.
 
### 2.3 Authorization Term
This Authorization is effective as of the date signed below and shall remain in effect for one (1) academic school year unless earlier terminated by either party. The School may renew this Authorization annually by executing a new signed copy. If the School does not renew this Authorization upon expiration, the School's authorized users may continue to access the platform under the abcAssess Terms of Service as individual subscribers, but the institutional authorization and COPPA consent provided by this document will no longer be in effect. abcAssess reserves the right to update the terms of this Authorization upon thirty (30) days written notice to the School at the email address provided in Section 1.

## Section 3: Privacy Authorization and Acknowledgments
 
### 3.1 Applicable Law
abcAssess serves educational institutions in the United States, Canada, Australia, New Zealand, the United Kingdom, and Ireland. The privacy framework applicable to this Authorization depends on the School's jurisdiction:
 
- **United States schools:** This Authorization serves as the School's COPPA consent, authorizing abcAssess to collect limited student information on behalf of parents and guardians for educational purposes consistent with COPPA, 15 U.S.C. § 6501 et seq., and FERPA, 20 U.S.C. § 1232g.
- **Canadian schools:** This Authorization is consistent with the Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act (PIPEDA) and applicable provincial privacy legislation.
- **Australian schools:** This Authorization is consistent with the Privacy Act 1988 (Cth) and the Australian Privacy Principles (APPs).
- **New Zealand schools:** This Authorization is consistent with the Privacy Act 2020.
- **UK and Ireland schools:** This Authorization is consistent with UK GDPR and EU GDPR respectively, and supplements the abcAssess Data Processing Agreement which governs processing under GDPR Article 28.
Equivalent student data protections apply to all schools regardless of jurisdiction. The checkbox acknowledgments in Section 3.2 apply to all schools and are consistent with the applicable privacy framework for each jurisdiction.
 
### 3.2 Acknowledgments
The Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA), 15 U.S.C. § 6501 et seq., restricts the online collection of personal information from children under the age of 13. Under COPPA, schools may serve as the authorized agent for parents or guardians, consenting to the collection of limited student information for educational purposes on their behalf. Equivalent protections apply under the privacy frameworks listed in Section 3.1 for international schools.

## Section 4: School Obligations
 
By executing this Authorization, the School agrees to the following ongoing obligations:
 
- Ensure that only personnel authorized under Section 2.2 access the abcAssess platform using the School's account credentials.
- Ensure that authorized users do not share login credentials with unauthorized individuals.
- Ensure that authorized users do not enter prohibited data categories as listed in Section 3.2 into the platform.
- Notify abcAssess promptly at legal@abcassess.app if the School becomes aware of: any unauthorized access to abcAssess accounts associated with the School; any suspected data breach involving student data processed through abcAssess; or any complaint or inquiry from a parent, guardian, or regulatory authority relating to student data in abcAssess.
- Maintain appropriate internal data governance policies governing the use of third-party educational technology tools and ensure compliance with applicable national, state, and provincial student data privacy laws.
- Upon termination of this Authorization or the School's use of abcAssess, export any student data the School wishes to retain and request deletion of remaining data in accordance with the abcAssess Privacy Policy by contacting legal@abcassess.app.
---
 
## Section 5: Prohibited Uses
 
The School agrees that authorized users will not use abcAssess to:
 
- Enter, upload, or store any student data beyond the categories authorized in Section 3.2.
- Upload photographs, video recordings, or audio recordings depicting or generated by students.
- Attempt to access another teacher's or school's data within the platform.
- Share assessment data with unauthorized third parties outside the School.
- Use the platform in any manner that violates FERPA, COPPA, or applicable national, state, or provincial student data privacy law.
- Attempt to reverse-engineer, circumvent, or otherwise interfere with the platform's security architecture.
